# My Sales Context

## Company
We sell an automation platform for mid-market B2B companies.
Our product replaces manual data entry and syncs across CRM, ERP, and accounting tools.

## Pricing
- Starter: $99/month (1 integration, 1 user)
- Growth: $299/month (3 integrations, 5 users)
- Enterprise: custom pricing

## Differentiators
- Live in 1 week (competitors take 3-6 months)
- No-code setup
- SOC2 compliant
- 10x cheaper than Workato/Zapier for high-volume ops

## Common objections and responses
- "We already use Zapier" → "Zapier breaks at scale. We handle 100x the volume at half the price."
- "We need to check with IT" → "We offer a 2-week sandbox pilot. IT can evaluate without any commitment."
- "It's too expensive" → "You're spending $4,000/month on manual work. We cost $299. That's 13x ROI."

## Closing triggers
- If they mention a timeline or deadline → push for pilot this week
- If they ask about implementation → they're ready, go for the close
- If they compare to competitors → highlight speed + cost advantage

## Tone
Direct, confident, numbers-driven. Never pushy. Always offer a concrete next step.
