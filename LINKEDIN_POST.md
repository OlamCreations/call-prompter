## Post (EN)

---

I built something I've wanted for years.

Call Prompter: a real-time AI co-pilot for Google Meet calls.

While you're talking to a prospect, AI analyzes the conversation every 15 seconds and streams:

→ What you should say right now
→ Buying signals detected
→ Objections and how to counter them
→ The exact moment to close
→ Flight signals ("I need to check with my partner")

Terminal-style UI. Black background. Text scrolls. You glance at your second screen while talking.

Green = prospect speaking.
Gold = close now.
Red = they're disengaging.

Runs locally. Your data stays on your machine. No cloud, no subscription.

Open source. MIT. Free.

→ github.com/OlamCreations/call-prompter
